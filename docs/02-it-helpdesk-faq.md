# IT Helpdesk FAQ

> _Last updated: 2023-09-18 · Owner: IT Operations_

This page covers laptop setup, accounts, and network access for new joiners.

## How do I get my accounts?

Your manager files a provisioning ticket before your start date. On day one you
should have email, Slack, and Okta. If anything is missing, email
**it-help@internal.example**.

## How do I connect to internal systems and staging?

You'll need the **company VPN** to reach internal environments.

1. Install the **GlobalConnect VPN** client from the Self-Service app.
2. Email **it-help@internal.example** to request your **staging credentials**
   (username + temporary password). Allow up to 2 business days.
3. Connect to the VPN, then log in to staging with the credentials IT sends you.

> If you are working remotely, the VPN is **required** for staging and all
> internal tools.

## How do I get a new laptop or peripherals?

File a request in the IT Self-Service app. Standard hardware ships within a week.

## Password resets

Use the Okta self-service reset, or email **it-help@internal.example** if you're
locked out.
