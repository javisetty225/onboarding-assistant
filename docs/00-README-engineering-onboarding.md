## Your first week, roughly

1. Get your accounts provisioned (see `02-it-helpdesk-faq.md`).
2. Get access to **staging** and clone the service you'll work on (see `01-environments-and-access.md`).
3. Read the code review rules before opening your first PR (see `03-code-review-and-prs.md`).
4. Find your team's playbook and your service's owner (see `04` and `05`).
